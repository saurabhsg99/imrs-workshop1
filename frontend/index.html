About the Conference

The International Mathematics Research Society (IMRS) works to bring together mathematicians, researchers, and scholars from around the world.

Our goal is to support the growth of mathematical knowledge through quality research, new ideas, and meaningful discussions.

We provide a platform where people can share their work, explore the latest discoveries, and promote the use of mathematics in science, engineering, and technology. IMRS also encourages working across different fields to solve real-world problems using mathematics.

We welcome high-quality original research papers and review articles that offer meaningful contributions to interdisciplinary research and its real-world applications across various domains.

Our Vision

  • To promote collaboration across different areas of mathematics and related fields.
  • To share new and important mathematical research with the global community.
  • To build a strong network of young and experienced mathematicians.
  • To inspire creative mathematical ideas that help solve real-world challenges

Topics Covered

The conference invites original research papers in both Pure and Applied Mathematics, with special emphasis on the following areas:
  • Algebra and Cryptography
  • Operator Theory and Operations Research
  • Fluid Mechanics and Biomathematics
  • Artificial Intelligence & Machine Learning
Submissions related to other emerging areas in Pure and Applied Mathematics are also encouraged.
